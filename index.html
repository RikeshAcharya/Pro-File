Why I do this work

AI Safety Mission

My work centres on developing reliable, interpretable artificial intelligence systems that remain aligned with human values — not just today, but as these systems grow more capable.

I believe that the most important question in technology right now is not what AI can do, but what it should do — and who gets to decide.

From Kathmandu, I work to ensure that AI safety research reflects diverse perspectives and serves all of humanity, not just those at the frontier.

Alignment

Ensuring AI systems pursue goals that reflect genuine human values.

Interpretability

Making model decisions understandable and auditable by people.

Robustness

Building systems that behave reliably even under adversarial conditions.

Ethics

Deploying intelligent systems with fairness and accountability at the core.
